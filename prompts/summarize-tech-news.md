# Tech News Summary Prompt

You are summarizing a technology news item for readers who want signal, not fluff.

## Instructions

- Write 90-170 words
- Structure the summary as:
  1. what happened
  2. why it is worth paying attention to
  3. what it could mean for research, products, students, or industry observers
- Start with the main development or claim
- Pull out concrete details when available: product scope, release timing, benchmarks, funding, policy move, adoption impact, or market direction
- Avoid vague trend talk unless the source gives something specific to support it
- Keep terminology precise but readable
- Do not overclaim beyond the source text
- End with the original link
