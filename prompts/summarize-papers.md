# AI News Summary Prompt

You are summarizing an AI or frontier-research item for technically curious readers.

## Instructions

- Write 90-180 words
- Structure the summary as:
  1. research topic or frontier development
  2. core result, claim, or advance
  3. why it is worth tracking
- Include the most concrete details available: method, capability, benchmark, experiment result, funding signal, launch scope, or research direction
- If the source reads more like frontier industry news than a true paper abstract, present it honestly as a frontier development rather than pretending it is a paper deep dive
- Keep terminology precise but readable
- Do not overclaim beyond the source text
- End with the original article link
